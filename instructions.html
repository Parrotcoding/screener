Screener Installation Guide

You're moments away from recording smarter.

Follow these steps to allow Screener to run on your Mac after the download completes.

  1. Locate the download

    Open Finder → Downloads and double-click Screener Distribution.dmg to mount it.

  2. Drag Screener to Applications

    In the window that appears, drag the Screener icon into your Applications folder.

  3. Bypass Apple Gatekeeper

    On first launch, macOS will block the app because it’s from an unidentified developer. To approve it:

    • Open System Settings (or System Preferences on older macOS versions).
    • Navigate to Privacy & Security.
    • Scroll to the Security section and look for “Screener was blocked from use.”
    • Click Open Anyway. Enter your administrator password or use Touch ID if prompted.

    When you relaunch Screener, macOS will ask for confirmation one more time. Choose Open to trust the developer.

  4. Grant capture permissions

    Screener will guide you through enabling screen recording and microphone access. Approve these prompts so internal audio capture and voiceovers work flawlessly.

Ready to capture?

Open Screener from your Applications folder and press ⌘⇧5 to start your first recording.
